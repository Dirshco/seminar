A Practical Workshop

This interactive seminar is designed to equip International Medical Graduates (IMGs) with the foundational knowledge and practical skills to successfully plan, conduct, and publish a systematic review. Explore the agenda and key topics below.

Seminar Agenda (3 Hours)

Seminar Modules

The seminar is divided into five core modules, each focusing on a critical stage of the systematic review process. Each module includes a presentation and a hands-on small group activity to reinforce learning. Click on any module to see more details.

Meet the Speakers

Our seminar features eight expert speakers, each bringing a wealth of experience in clinical research and systematic reviews. They will guide you through each module and are available for questions throughout the workshop.
